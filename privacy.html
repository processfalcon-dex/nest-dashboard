Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026 · Last Updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Process Falcon, LLC ("Process Falcon," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the SolarNest Dashboard application ("the App"), including its optional Gmail integration.

1. What the App Does

SolarNest Dashboard is an internal operations tool for solar installation companies. It displays project pipeline data from Glide and, optionally, unread emails from a connected Gmail inbox — all within the user's browser.

2. Gmail Data

If you choose to connect your Gmail account, the App requests read-only access to your Gmail inbox using Google's OAuth 2.0 authorization. Specifically:

  • We request the gmail.readonly scope to fetch your unread email subjects, senders, dates, and preview snippets.
  • This data is fetched directly from Google's API in your browser and displayed on screen.
  • We do not transmit, store, or log your Gmail data on any server. Your emails never leave your browser session.
  • Your OAuth access token is stored only in your browser's sessionStorage and is discarded when you close the tab.
  • You can disconnect Gmail at any time by clicking your email address in the dashboard header.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Process Falcon does not:

  • Store, transmit, or share your Gmail messages or metadata
  • Use your Gmail data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share your Gmail data with any third party
  • Retain any Gmail data after your browser session ends

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

SolarNest Dashboard's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Project Data (Glide)

Pipeline and project data is fetched from Glide using your organization's API credentials. This data is displayed within the dashboard and is not stored, logged, or transmitted beyond what is necessary to render the interface.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses sessionStorage to temporarily store your Gmail access token for the duration of your browser session. No cookies are set. No persistent identifiers are stored.

7. Who Can Use This App

Access to SolarNest Dashboard is restricted to authorized users added by Process Falcon or the subscribing organization. This is not a public application.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
